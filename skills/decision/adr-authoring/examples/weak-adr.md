# ADR-007: Database Choice

**Status:** Accepted

## Context

We need a database.

## Decision

We will use MongoDB.

## Consequences

It will be good for our project.

---

## Why this is weak

1. **Vague context** — "We need a database" gives no information about constraints, scale, or requirements
2. **No alternatives** — Was anything else considered? Why MongoDB over PostgreSQL, DynamoDB, etc.?
3. **No negative consequences** — Every choice has tradeoffs. "It will be good" is not analysis.
4. **Missing date and deciders** — Who made this decision? When? Can't trace accountability.
5. **No specific requirements** — What properties does the database need? Schema flexibility? Transactions? Scale?
